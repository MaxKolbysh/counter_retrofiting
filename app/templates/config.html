How it works
  • Raw Image: Direct capture from Pi Camera 2.
  • Processed Image: Grayscale + Adaptive Thresholding applied to highlight the mechanical numbers.
  • OCR Result: The numbers extracted by Tesseract OCR using a digits-only whitelist.

If numbers aren't detected correctly, try adjusting the lighting or the distance of the camera from the meter.
